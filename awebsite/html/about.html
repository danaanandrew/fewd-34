Shy Gangsta is an American west coast artist, writer and photographer working in the mediums of digital and film photography, printmaking, watercolor and digital media. Constantly moving, always inspired, the guy who holds a beer and stands in the corner of the room at a party, shy but still a G at heart. Shy Gangsta is available for photography, writing and art commissions on request.
